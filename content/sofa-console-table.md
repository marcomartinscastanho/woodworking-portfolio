My first project that resembles something professional.

I designed it on a sketch software, used proper wood (not pallet wood) and created a [program to plot the pieces](https://github.com/marcomartinscastanho/woodworking-project-plotter) to cut from the panels.

The base was designed as 2 pieces to make it easier to transport, but the single-piece top board makes it look like a single object.

Made from oak.
